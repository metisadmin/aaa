NHRSP | National Health Research and Studies Portal

Welcome to NHRSP

The National Health Research and Studies Portal (NHRSP) is a database for national health research data, it provides a platform for researchers to share and access research data, to facilitate the reuse of existing health research data in accordance to the ethical standards of scientific research.

The Center for Health Research Studies at the Saudi Health Council seeks to cultivate an open research data environment, believing that sharing research data accelerates science by facilitating collaborative relationships, scientific transparency and reproducibility. NHRSP encourages scientific research and support researchers to bridge knowledge gaps and generate new research ideas.

NHRSP aims to:
  • Provide a platform for researchers to share and access national health research data
  • Provide support for data management and data sharing across the Kingdom
  • Establish a national health research repository
  • Promote international data sharing practices across the Kingdom
